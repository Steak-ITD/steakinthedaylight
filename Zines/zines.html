Misery and Misanthropy Zine

A zine made in 2022, at the peak of my sadness. One of them, anyway. A series of kind-of disconnected thoughts put into visual form via collage and drawing. It's been described to me as maximalist. Slight warning for crudely drawn violence and small sexuality at the end. Links to print versions can be found here!

Shifting Gardens

This is an experimental fanzine of artwork and text featuring grass-type Pokemon in various perilous situations. Not all of them will necessarily feature gore, but a theme in all of them is some sort of body modification/desecration. Features 6 images with text to go along.

I've attempted to iframe the pdf into the is page, however there is no zoom out function and this zine has full page spreads. So, it is best viewed downloaded, or printed, from here!
