Wintry Romp

This was drawn for me by Avvy. Featuring an idea I really enjoy for some reason: Polyantha having sex with wild animals. We decided on goats in a winter environment being led to a farm.

Her description: In the thick of winter, the goats don't get to go romp about to play about the fields all that often, so Polyantha takes it upon herself to give them some playtime. Enamored with her as ever, they follow her over bridges over frozen ponds, traipsing the dense snowy landscape together, dozens of animals only gazing forward at her next step. They'll be taken back into the barn after a little exercise! But the rowdy and pent-up goats have their own minds set... Even the littlest fellas are too pushy for her to do anything about-- their little nips are intimidating, and she doesn't want to fight a little goat...! What to do... ? She's never been a very resilient or confident girl. They're...just trying to play, aren't they? She's frozen in action as her knees sink into the cold snow with a forceful shove... Hoo....
