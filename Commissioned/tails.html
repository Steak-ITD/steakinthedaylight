Tails

This was drawn for me by Bird. I asked him to draw Tails with a vagina using his fingers and a toy to masturbate for the first time.
With maturing comes new feelings, new urges. Being as curious as he is, Miles wants to do research into these feelings to understand them, and perhaps understand himself better as well. So he "performs" experiments with himself, figuring out how he reacts to different stimulus both physically and mentally. Still being new to this kind of thing, the first times are quite overwhelming for him on both fronts, almost causing a panic, but ultimately resulting in a state of calmness and relief.
