A version of Kitsunami the Fennec for my classic Sonic universe.

Biography

He is a hydrolic android who's true name is Wave Unit 21. He comes from Little Planet's good future, invented to test hydraulic/hydroelectric technology. He knows almost nothing about his past or what he was made for at first. He is a force to be reckoned with; not easy to convince, cold, and a bit coniving and insidious. He's also polite, respectful, and loyal to a fault. He will repay any debt he feels he's owed.

He makes water for himself by absorbing molecules in the air into his pack, and then he can shape the water into some form, usually tails, sometimes hands, or spears. The water is typically cold but gets hotter with his emotion, or it can become ice or steam. Freshwater is preferred mainly due to taste.

Relationships

He choses to follow Surge as he loved the philosophy she lives by. They are very loyal to each other, even if they argue sometimes.
