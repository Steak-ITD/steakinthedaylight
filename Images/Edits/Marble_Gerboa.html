Marble Gerboa

originally designed by CRUSH

Marble is a hybrid of a jerboa and a gecko. Its colors are based on sunsets.

Its demeanor is curious but timid. Very much a flight or fighter. Reacts very positively to being treated kindly and very poorly to being threatened. And it has a good memory, so it will keep in mind who did what. Very affectionate once you get to know it. It smiles by showing a lot of teeth. Attacks with their claws and feet, and can screech very loudly. It likes to eat beetles, those brown bugs with green blood are its favorite food.

It has rodent-like hemipenes. It masturbates frequently to calm down. Could use the tip of its tail to do so, or hump soft surfaces or objects, like sand. It prefers bottoming; it would like having its hemipenes stimulated directly, by hand(or equivalent), or mouth.
