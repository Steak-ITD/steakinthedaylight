Steak's Galaxy

My own personal galaxy and its inhabitants. Very undeveloped as of now.

Cosmoton

The most populated planet in the galaxy, full of many diverse species and cultures. Also has many legends surrounding it, inculding those of deities and aliens. Has 1 sun and 1 moon.

Sphinx Planet

A planet primarily populated by Sphinxes, creatures with humanoid faces and varied animal bodies. Consists entirely of an ocean and islands of various sizes. Has 1 sun and no moon. The flora are trees and plants that bear fruit, and grass. Sphinxes are herbivores or monivores. Aquatic and terrestial sphinxes don't get along and will eat each other.

Sphinx genetics seem to have no rhyme or reason, and traits appear at random. Some traits are more desirable than others depending on the island and its culture. The truth is that the sphinxes were not spawned on this planet organically; they are an experiment from a long lost civilization. Nobody in the galaxy knows what the purpose of said experiments were, and this fact is lost to time.

The economy is based on trading items and favors. "Status" is denoted by wearing accessories. Being "rich" doesn't make one much less vulnerable, and items will be pawned off when the owner dies.
