Myth is a mysterious Sphinx living somewhere remote on the planet.

Biography

Myth seems to be half dead both in demeanor and body composition. The first half of his body looks to be somewhat decayed, while the second is epherial and defies logic. He is apparently revered as some sort of Godlike figure on the planet, despite very few ever seeing him or knowing about his condition. He may have been a failed experiment from the creator of the Sphinxes, but his purpose is unknown, of course.

Relationships

Probably has the most relations with the other Sphinxes on his remote island, who worship him due to his mysterious nature. He doesn't seem to mind that much but isn't seeking them out either.
