Floral Fae is an alien living secretly on Cosmoton.

Biography

She's a benevolent spirit with a spicy side. She'd been looking for a new home after her own had been ruined by a red giant star. She's the one who created the alien sanctuary on the Planet.

She has been lonely for a long time and as a result is friendly to a fault, ready to welcome anyone needing escape from their former homes and retreating from destroyed planets. She has also been rumored to be rather promiscuous, though some say it's another result of her loneliness and desire to not be the last of her species anymore.

Relationships

She has many friendly relationships with the other aliens in her sanctuary.

Appearance

Complexion:

Her skin is a light teal green color.

Facial features:

Her face resembles a spider. She has six eyes. She also has leaves in her fur to match her antennae.

Hair:

Most fur is on her head and around her neck, with the head fur being light green and the neck fur being dark green with pink accents.

Body:

She has eight limbs like a spider, but is more insect shaped, including her wings.
