A fussy and stuck up sphinx. She hops around like a rabbit and has humanoid hands that let her pick things up easily. Despite her fussiness she is very patient outwardly in getting whatever she desires. She enjoys yellow and golden things and hordes anything with that color. She also has a strange pet called Paplone whom she adores.
