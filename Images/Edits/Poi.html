A unique creature from the planet of sphinxes.

Biography

A sphinx living on a "less civilized" island full of wild animals. She has multiple unique traits that would be seen as odd on other islands, such as a complete lack of eyes. She frequently injures herself while hunting and has an unstable gait as a result. Her means of defense are the spikes on her head, which have poison.

Relationships

Has very few relationships due to being on an isolated island.
