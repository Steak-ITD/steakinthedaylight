Desmair

designed anonymously

Desmair is a zombie pony that is countless years old, yet still acts somewhat like a bratty child. It thinks mainly on instincts and gets very frustrated if its in a state it doesn't like. It's very fussy about weather and only wants to be in cold temperatures and wet areas. Its movements are erratic and sudden.

Its horn is functional and is mainly used for levitation of itself or other objects. It takes great pride in how it looks and of course gets fusst about being dirty or too wet. It is concerned about falling apart as it is not physically strong and is slightly rotting.
