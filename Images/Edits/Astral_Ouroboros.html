Astral Ouroboros is a cosmic creature living within Cosmoton's moon.

Biography

The inhabitants of Cosmoton are unsure if it is even real or not; and among those who believe it, they are unsure of its effects. Does it cause madness in creatures? Do the supermoons contribute to natural disasters? Is it responsible for the cause of the craters on the moon, or the colors it changes to? Many conspiracies surround it.

It was created alongside the planets moon and is intrinsically connected to it. The moon's health is its health, so it is equally as indestructible (nukes will not harm it). As such it can't move that far from the moon or the planet it rotates around.

Relationships

It might have been seen by some aliens passing by Cosmoton. It's friendly enough to make itself known and say hi.

Appearance

Complexion

Bright white scales matching the light reflected on the moon. Also accented by dark blue stars and crater-like markings.

Facial features

Deep blue eyes, and more star marks under them.

Body

A thick serpentine body. Has violet blue wings on the back of its head.
