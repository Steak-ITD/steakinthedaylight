Astral Ouroboros

designed anonomously

Astral Ouroboros is a cosmic snake being living within the planet's moon. The inhabitants are unsure if it is even real or not; and among those who believe it, they are unsure of its effects. Does it cause madness in creatures? Do the supermoons contribute to natural disasters? Is it responsible for the cause of the craters on the moon, or the colors it changes to? Many conspiracies surround it.

It was created alongside the planets moon and is intrinsically connected to it. The moon's health is its health, so it is equally as indestructible (nukes will not harm it). As such it can't move that far from the moon or the planet it rotates around.
