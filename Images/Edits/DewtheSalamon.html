Dew the Salamon

original design by Avvy

Dew is a delicate Salamon, with a dewlap and lop ears, resembling a rabbit more than a dog. Dew is easy to subdue. It uses magical attacks mainly due to having a weak disposition and no claws. It can only reach an adult/champion stage before feeling too overwhelmed to go further.
