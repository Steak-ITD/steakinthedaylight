Aniki #7043

Labbit species by doctor

Aniki is a labbit who loves to draw. She keeps pencils and other art supplies inside of her pouch. Drawing is her way of destressing. However, her own sells her drawings as a novelty. When she protests, her memories and emotions are wiped. This happens frequently, but the effects are not well known. Sometimes her tails lets out tears.
