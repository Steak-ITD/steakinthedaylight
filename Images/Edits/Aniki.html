Aniki is a regular Lab Rabbit.

Biography

Aniki gets stress easily, and draws many pictures to help herself calm down. However, her owner sells her drawings as a novelty. When she protests, her memories and emotions are wiped. This happens frequently, but the effects are not well known.

Relationships

She has an owner who does not care much about her feelings.
