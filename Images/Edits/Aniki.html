Aniki is a regular Lab Rabbit.

Biography

Aniki gets stress easily, and draws many pictures to help herself calm down. However, her owner sells her drawings as a novelty. When she protests, her memories and emotions are wiped. This happens frequently, but the effects are not well known.

Relationships

She has an owner who does not care much about her feelings.

Appearance

Facial features

Small eyes with large lashes. A sharp buck tooth.

Hair

White arms and legs, light blue feet and tail. Indigo torso and pouch.

Body

Pretty thin around the body and tail.

Trivia

Hobbies

Drawing is her way of destressing. She keeps pencils and other art supplies inside of her pouch.

None.

Low and shy.
