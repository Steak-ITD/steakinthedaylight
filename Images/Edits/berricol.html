Berricol is a calm, subtle, slow creature.

Biography

It rarely emotes physically or facially. It can be seen walking on two legs and standing on four. It likes to walk outdoors in wide open areas, roll around in grass, and stare at clouds. While not spooked by loud noises, it will leave any area with them.

Its limbs and back are flexible, and it can bend into many positions. It could move faster if it chose to, but prefers to move steadily and slowly, with the exception of swimming. It likes having its feet massaged.

Relationships

It has a weird relationship with Desmair. Desmair seems to be both fascinated and frazzled by Berricol's existence, touching its body repeatedly in ways most would find invasive. Berricol doesn't care that much, and this frustrates Desmair even more...
