Shiplume

A digital only drawing. I sketched the shapes, then painted them. I usually do painting in Krita, so this time I tried out brushes in Clip Studio Paint. The textures of such can be seen mostly in Shiplume's fur. I also tried out the chromatic aberration feature, which is better than I thought it would be.
