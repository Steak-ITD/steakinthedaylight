Eulipotyplhan

Sometimes people call Sonic a "rodent". But actually hedgehogs are not in the order Rodentia, they are in Eulipotyphla. I am ashamed of this cartoon video game about talking animals for not getting its zoology correct.
In all seriousness I don't like how this picture came out. It wasn't as fun to make as I hoped and some details bug me, but I spent my whole day drawing so may as well keep it.
