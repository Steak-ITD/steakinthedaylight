Deirdre Dragon Concept

Design for an AU where Deirdre is a demon dragon creature and Sigurd searches for her, still in love and still a fool. She believes she is too dangerous to be around, and almost lets him kill her with a sword, until he recognized her at the last second. He wants to convince her she is worth loving.

I'm a cheesy romantic. Anyway, this design is based on medieval European dragons.
