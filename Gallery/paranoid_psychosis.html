Paranoid Psychosis

The second piece I made for Pico Day 2021. I made this one on a whim when I discovered an old flash from 1999 on the Wayback machine. The flash has a list of the main trio's characteristics, and I found it pretty intriguing. Pico is described as an insomniac, obsessive, homicidal, and schizophrenic (also a wannabe DJ). I would be interested in seeing more content that explores these aspects of his personality.
