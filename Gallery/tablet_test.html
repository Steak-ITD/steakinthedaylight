Tablet Tests

Testing out how I want to use my graphic tablet in the future. For the top 2 I had a fixed zoom because I had to charge the tablet, and zooming with it is very hard. The top on I drew at 100%, the other at 50%. Lines and coloring were much easier the closer I was zoomed in. The bottom 2 I did first, and the lines turned out really wonky so I also scribbled the colors. Also, using the stabilizer didn't make any noticable difference.
