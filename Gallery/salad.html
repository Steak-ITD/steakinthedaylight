Salad

An idea I've had since Halloween of last year; a Saplant being cut apart and leaking veggie juices. It's a non descript specimen.

I haven't been motivated to draw since school ended, so it was a shock I managed to finish this. I don't usually finish pencil drawings, but I wasn't particularly feeling like doing digital work, so I had fun with my box of 100 Crayola colored pencils.
