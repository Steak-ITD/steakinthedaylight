Reese Poetry

Reese Puffs, great food/ peanut butter chocolate/ the best of breakfast

Eat it every day/ the sweet combo of flavors/ just makes my morning

candy and sugar/ Not good for your dental health/ but good for tastebuds

oh no there is much/ bought too many at the store/ time for halloween

they all vanish first/ everybody loves these things/ but no more for me

have to get more of/ my favorite combo sweet treat/ let's go to the store

sold out they tell me/it's a very loved candy/ bought all on day one

now what do i do/ distressed over the loss of/ my sweet confection

i can't recover/ from this horrible shortage/ oh wait some skittles
