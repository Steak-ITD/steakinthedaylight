Headcanons: Boyfriend and Girlfriend

  • He likes listening to music, singing, adventuring, and writing. Best at focusing on these things.

  • Doesn't like talking out loud, especially with people he isn't close to. Will try to avoid it by making nonsensical noises.

  • Hums to himself a lot, plays with his pockets a lot.

  • Likely has a song stuck in his head at all times. Tends to get lost in his thoughts.

  • In general, he doesn't fear many things. Confidently dives head first into danger if it might be fun/in the way. Not realizing something is dangerous in the first place.

  • Sudden loud noises bother him immensely (thunder, large bells, etc.)

  • Somewhat literal minded and might not pick up on metaphors or euphemisms in speech.

  • Though not very talkative he can write very eloquently and rhythmically.

  • He doesn't like being touched for long periods of time. This is one of the few things that will make him nervous. With the exception of one person.

  • He is very resourceful with his voice. Though high, it has a decent range.

  • Tends to forget belongings, and picks up weird objects.

  • Has a hard time giving attention to things he isn't interested in (subjects in school).

  • She is just as fearless (and dumb) as him and more outwardly reckless because she likely has actual means of protecting herself

  • Always in motion. Fidgets when standing still, or moves along to a tune, in or outside of her head. She likes to dance.

  • Impatient, doesn't like waiting for long periods of time and groans a lot when bored.

  • Somewhat talkative, when she gets excited she can speak for a long time. Going on tangents or getting off topic.

  • Takes petty insults too personally and is prone to tears.

  • Dislikes small spaces/being restrained as she likes having room to move.

  • Likes scary and weird things like large spiders and centipedes and dark alleyways.

  • Usually using glamour to appear more human. This is because of “true love”. Seems like she wants to appear more average for her lover, possibly separate herself from her parents.

  • Also dislikes loud and sudden noises/discordant music.

  • She is enamored with his writing and they like to write love letters to each other.

  • She also encourages him to sing more. He tries to do the same but she doesn't seem very fond of her own voice.

  • He admires her power and beauty and thinks she's very talented with music, and would like to see her express it more

  • He finds it much easier to talk to her than anyone else. She's the only person he'll let physically close to him. Enthusiastically, even.
