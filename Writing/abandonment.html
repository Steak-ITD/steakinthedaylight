Notes

Intentionally vague and nebulous creepy drabble I did in order to remove it from my head. Written before the one where Tails eats Sonic...

The small room was a mess. Pieces of broken furniture and ripped cloth strewn about; the carpet was stained in various reds. 

"What happened in here? Where's Sonic?" Knuckles yelled.

Tails didn't reply. He kept staring at the wall.

Impatient, Knuckles yelled again, "Answer me! What happened!?"

"I begged," he said, voice low.

"Begged...?"

"I begged, pleaded with him to run away. Told him I couldn't control myself anymore," he intoned. "But he wouldn't listen to me. He stayed."

He moves for the first time in hours, looking down at his hands.

"He said, 'I won't abandon you.'"

Carpeted in red, dotted with blue.

"He was a great friend."
