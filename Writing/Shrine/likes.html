Video Games: Commercial

  • Paper Mario The Thousand Year Door - Simple and fun gameplay loops, iconic art styles, charming cast of characters, cute storytelling
  • Pokemon Generation 4 - near the peak of Pokémon. Storytelling and lore become stronger, map and towns are memorable, music becomes more complex
  • Pokémon Generation 5 - the peak of Pokémon. Experimented in character and storytelling to good effect, Pokémon designs are distinct, great music for each area, gameplay is faster
  • Sonic the Hedgehog CD - beautiful visual storytelling that emphasises nature and technology combination, unique level design, amazing soundtrack
  • Siactro - Developer making 90's inspired games with a focus on exploration and speed. Charming character design, fun level design, mysterious and cute set design
  • Densetsu no Stafy/ The Legend of Starfy - perfected the concept of water levels, cool character designs, good difficulty curve
  • Monster Crown https://store.steampowered.com/app/830370/Monster_Crown/ - quality breeding mechanic that allows for high customization of monsters, many monster forms in general, memorable designs, easy to understand map, although very buggy
  • Puyo Puyo Fever 2 - fun puzzle game with town map and lore exploration in items and library
  • Cavern of Dreams https://store.steampowered.com/app/2059660/Cavern_of_Dreams- beautiful nostalgic collection game, cute world and characters
  • Moonstone Island https://store.steampowered.com/app/1658150/Moonstone_Island/ - farming sim combined with monster taming amd exploration of magic world
  • Slime Rancher https://store.steampowered.com/app/433340/Slime_Rancher/ - exploration filled raising game with fun gameplay loop (beware motion sickness
  • Sonic Mania https://store.steampowered.com/app/584400 - anniversary game that perfectly captures the charm of classic sonic, also robust modding community
