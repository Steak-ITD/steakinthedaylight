Based on one of my headcanons (Girlfriend does not like having her movement restricted) and a nightmare.

Girlfriend hated being tied up like this.

It was like being stuck in a tiny room while still outside, freedom seemingly just in reach. But her upper body was restrained.

She couldn't move away to a better place. She couldn't get away from the creatures toying with her clothes. She could only struggle while they held her flailing legs. She could only shudder as a dry finger slowly rubbed against her vagina.

"This is all it takes to get you wet?" They mocked. "What a pathetic demon."

She sobbed and closed her eyes. She was unable to do anything else.
