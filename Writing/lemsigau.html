Lemsig AUs

This ship generally contains some sort of age gap that may vary between AUs.

Collection of alternate universes for my ship, Sig x Lemres.

Original Canon

Lemres has always been careful towards Sig and his situation. Sig is suspicious of him though. I imagine he will get to know him better soon. And realize Lemres is kind of sheltered and isn't aware how he comes off to others, and they're similar in that way. They are also both free spirits at heart. I like how their character songs are both about exploration in different vibes. One about doing so at your own slow pace while everyone else moves so fast. The other about trying not to be afraid of what you find and learn what you're looking for. I think they'll rub off on each other.

Reverse/Increased Age Gap

Lemres is a lonely boy with few friends who finds Sig to be an interesting change of pace from his normal life and admires how he follows his passion freely. Sig is an introvert adult also with few friends who find Lem annoying at first but becomes more familiar with him and his situation and grows to like him more. I guess it's not super different from the regular version but the bigger age difference does change the dynamic a bit, no? In this version Sig has more of the "caretaker" role being older and more "put together" so to speak. He wants Lemres to learn to be more self accepting, independent, not so nervous all the time. And Lemres does feel more free to be himself around Sig. He's a soothing presence.

I'm imagining their ages as 8-9 for Lem and about 25 for Sig, and their appearance is based on the mini/mega forms from PP7 respectively.

Darkling Demon

This AU features Dark/Black Sig. Lemres and Akuma are his caretakers and keep him from falling into chaos like his other half.

Sig still has his other friends, and his state makes him easier to understand a little. He is more visibly and audibly emotional. He is still blunt though, stating his feeling and thought plainly. It's a bit of a shock to him.

His state runs in his family and so he is more aware of his heritage as a half-demon, but not of being a reincarnation of the original demon's body... I don't have any frame of reference for his family. Perhaps something happened to them to put him in the care of Lemres and Akuma. Maybe they were evil. Maybe both.

I still think of him as autistic, and very very particular about touching. It's overwhelming to be touched on his non demon skin, it makes him shiver and shrink away. Only the two people closest to him can touch him there and even then it has to be light and soft, like how a bug feels crawling on him. He likes when bugs crawl on him, as well as the buzzing noises they make, and is extra gentle with them.

He feels most attached to Lem in the way they do because he's known him the longest, he's doted over him for years, so he doesn't have much reason not to trust him more than canon (but he still thinks his habits are weird). They are both also careful bout their friends regularly, so I think that it rubs off on Sig more. He would be like the kind of embarrassing dad of his friend group.

Sig can be kind of handsy with Lemres, being just about the only person he's close to. He has to be gentle though, his arms are so strong, he could hurt someone! But sometimes that extra... energy, builds up, and he feels these urges to scratch, and growl, and purr. Lem ends up being a target for this aggression, covered in bruises and scratches that he has to heal with magic. But he doesn't seem to mind much, seeing it as another way of helping. Yet he feels inclined to keep it a secret.

I think, if we go the route of Sig's parents being evil, Lemres feels more devoted to raising him well because he left his own family to follow his dreams, and he wants Sig to be able to do that too, live without that kind of pressure. He is encouraging of his admittedly small amount of habits.
