Puyo Short 5

kissing foodplay

When Lemres returned home, he immediately noticed a faint red glow from the hallway. Sig had been waiting for him. The red light moved closer, and he could see the desperation in his eyes. He opened his arms to embrace him.

As they hugged, Sig looked up. “I'm hungry,” he whined. Lemres smiled and pulled something out of his pocket; a peppermint candy. He placed it on his tongue and into his mouth, keeping his eyes on his partner.

“Don't be afraid,” he coos. Sig leaned up and kissed him. Their tongues met and played with each other and the candy between them. After a few minutes, their lips separated, leaving behind a trail of saliva. The candy had settled in Sig's mouth, and he audibly swallowed it. Lemres watched the thick piece slide down his throat. “Satisfied, little bug?” he asked. Seeing those colorful, ravenous eyes told him all he needed to know...

This made me feel so weird to write! It was hornier than sex.
