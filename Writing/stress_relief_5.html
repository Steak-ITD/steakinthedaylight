Revoked Consent

This is my least favorite, mainly because I think this concept deserves more than 100 words. I may expand on it one day.

"It wasn't so bad, was it?" Pico said once he got up. Boyfriend's eyes were shut. He was sniffling.

Pico sighed and got off the bed to clean himself. Shortly after, he heard a whimper. "Why..."

He didn't look back. "Why...?"

"Why wouldn't you s-stop...?" Boyfriend had started crying again.

Pico sighed again. He was being dramatic. He'd wanted this. He couldn't just change his mind in the middle, right?

He kept telling himself that. He wouldn't be sad forever. He would still trust him.

He turned back around, but Boyfriend shrank into the sheets. "No... don't come any closer."
