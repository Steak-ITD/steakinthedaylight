Stress Relief: Aftercare

PART 2 OF 5

Boyfriend sat up on the bed. He closed his eyes. The pain of the marks on his back ebbed throughout his body, in time with his heartbeat. He took a deep breath, and sobbed as he exhaled.

Girlfriend panicked when she heard it. "Are you ok? Was I too rough?"

He opened his eyes again. He crawled up to her and swung his arms around her neck. Hoarse, he whispered, "I'm fine. You were wonderful."

In turn she gently wrapped her arms around his waist. How strange, he thought. The same person's touch can be so terrifying and so sweet.
