It Should Have Been Easy

Not much to say about this. I just like writing drabbles. This was written in 2021 so, before much lore was established for this series.

It should have been easy.

Sure, Pico had a history with him. But it was just that. Past. It didn’t matter anymore.

He had a clear shot. That idiot probably wouldn’t move out of the way if he walked right up to him, gun in hand.

He should have jumped at the chance to kill him. And yet…

Even with the hatred he felt, the hurt at seeing him smile, just for a second, he couldn’t help but think back.

To when that smile was for him.

His finger hovered over the trigger.

It was never going to be easy.
